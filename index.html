Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

Hi, I’m Jonathan Clement, a soon-to-be graduate from Clayton State University with a passion for technology and problem-solving. My focus lies in database concepts like modeling, design, and querying, where I enjoy creating efficient and scalable solutions. Recently, I’ve expanded my skillset into web design and development through volunteer work with Securus House, a local domestic violence shelter, where I redesigned their website to enhance accessibility and functionality.
